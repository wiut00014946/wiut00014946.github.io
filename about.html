About Travel Blog

Travel Blog is a free online travel diary for travellers around the world. This blog is a web based journal with its content mostly focusing on travelling and adventures. In Travel Blog you can find many useful articles for those who travel a lot or are willing to travel in the nearest future.
Travel Blog is an advertising supported online blog. This means that we offer our services free of charge for users. Instead, we focus on advertising as the primary source of income. However, we offer premium (paid) subscription for VIP users. This subscription allows users enjoy our services without being distracted by advertisements.
In order to follow Travel Blog you only need stable internet connection and modern web browser. Let us handle rest of the job and enjoy your reading.
